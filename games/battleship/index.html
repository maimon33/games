How to Play — Battleship
  1. Placement: Select a ship, click your grid to place it. Use Rotate to change orientation. Place all 5 ships then click Ready.
  2. Battle: Click the enemy grid to fire a shot. 🔴 = hit, ✕ = miss.
  3. Sink all 5 enemy ships to win!
  4. Press Invite Friend to play online. Host places ships first.
