How to Play — Mastermind
  1. Crack the secret 4-color code. You have 10 attempts.
  2. Click a color swatch at the bottom to select it, then click the 4 holes to fill your guess.
  3. Press Submit (or the row arrow) when all 4 holes are filled.
  4. Black peg = right color, right position. White peg = right color, wrong position.
  5. Use the clues to narrow down the secret code!
