How to Play — Boggle
  1. Find as many words as possible in 3 minutes.
  2. Click and drag (or tap each letter) to trace a path through adjacent letters.
  3. Letters must be adjacent (including diagonals) and each cell can only be used once per word.
  4. Words must be at least 3 letters long.
  5. Scoring: 3–4 letters = 1 pt · 5 = 2 · 6 = 3 · 7 = 5 · 8+ = 11
