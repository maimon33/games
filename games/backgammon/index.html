How to Play — Backgammon
  1. White moves from point 24→1 (right to left on top, left to right on bottom). Black moves 1→24.
  2. Click Roll Dice on your turn. Then click a piece to select it, and click a valid destination.
  3. Landing on a single opponent piece hits it — it goes to the bar and must re-enter before other moves.
  4. When all your pieces are in your home board (points 1–6 for White, 19–24 for Black), you can start bearing off.
  5. First to bear off all 15 pieces wins!
  6. Press Invite Friend to play online. White (host) goes first.
