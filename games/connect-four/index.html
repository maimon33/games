How to Play — Connect Four
  1. Players alternate dropping pieces into any column.
  2. Pieces fall to the lowest empty row in that column.
  3. First to connect 4 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins!
  4. If the board fills up with no winner, it's a draw.
  5. Press Invite Friend to play online. Red (host) always goes first.
